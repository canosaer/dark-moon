About Dark Moon

Outwit, Outgun, Outfly

Take to the stars and trust no one as you race to recover alien technology from the moons of a distant planet. As the captain of your own ship, you'll hire a crew, buy weapons and upgrades, and vie with other captains for wealth and glory!

Dark Moon is a groundbreaking science fiction board game for 1-5 players. Make strategic decisions as you build your ship, negotiate and bluff about your intentions, and shoot your rivals out of the sky!

Groundbreaking Game Mechanics

The space captains of Dark Moon are freelancers in search of alien sites that are discovered each round on the moons of a distant planet. Each captain selects their destination in secret and reveals their choice simultaneously. When two captains choose the same destination, combat begins!

Combat in Dark Moon is based on a groundbreaking new system that is fast, furious, and fun! Will you outmanuever your opponent and fire on them with an autocannon? Perhaps you'll go in guns blazing with a particle beam, or fire a volley of missles and watch as they try in vain to evade certain doom!

Don't miss out on this original, thought-provoking game!

Shadow of the Dreadnaught

Not into competition and betrayal? Dark Moon also features solo and cooperative play in its Shadow of the Dreadnaught game mode.

In Shadow of the Dreadnaught, players work together to accumulate resources and upgrade their ships, all while battling automated enemies in service of the AI Dreadnaught.

Players beware - when the automated enemies gather resources for the Dreadnaught, it grows in power. At the end of the game, the players will go toe to toe with the Dreadnaught, and together they will either win or be destroyed!
